Mental Health Advocacy

For the past six years, I've dedicated myself to a mission: to break down the stigma surrounding mental health and offer support to those in need. My journey began with a simple yet powerful tool: social media. Through inspirational images and videos, I aimed to create a safe space where individuals could connect, find solace, and understand that they are not alone. I’ve worked tirelessly to reach individuals experiencing mental health struggles, especially those battling suicidal thoughts. The journey started with a deep desire to make a difference. I recognized that many people, especially in times of crisis, often feel alone and unheard. Social media offered a unique opportunity to connect with those who might otherwise feel isolated. By sharing uplifting content—whether a comforting quote, an empowering message, or a reminder that they are not alone—I’ve aimed to create a space of solace and understanding. But my commitment extends beyond online platforms. I actively provide live chat support to individuals experiencing suicidal crises. These interactions, while challenging, are incredibly rewarding. Witnessing someone move from despair to hope is an experience that fuels my passion and drives me to continue this work. Throughout the years, the most rewarding part of this journey has been seeing people’s lives change. I’ve had the privilege of connecting with countless individuals who have found healing, strength, and, in many cases, a reason to keep going. My advocacy is not just about raising awareness; it's about fostering a community of support and understanding. I believe that by sharing stories, offering a listening ear, and promoting self-care, we can create a world where mental health is prioritized and everyone has access to the support they deserve.

Projects

#Reach Out Social media campaign

A project where i created and shared a series of inspirational images and videos aimed at uplifting those struggling with mental health. Each post was designed to provide comfort, encouragement, and a reminder that individuals are not alone in their struggles. The campaign grew into a trusted source of hope for your followers, with content focused on mental wellness, resilience, and recovery..

Live Chat Support for Suicidal Crisis (24/7 Peer Support Initiative

A project where i offered real-time live chat support to individuals in suicidal crisis, providing a safe space for people to reach out during their most difficult moments. The initiative helped connect people with someone who could listen, provide emotional support, and guide them toward further resources for professional care. This direct support has saved lives and created an ongoing conversation about the importance of peer-based mental health care.
